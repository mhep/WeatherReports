Maximilian Gregor Hepach

is a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Potsdam and project coordinator of Weather Reports. He recently submitted his PhD thesis on the phenomenology of climate and its changes in Geography at the University of Cambridge. Most recently, he has published in Progress in Human Geography. His research interests include phenomenology, media theory, and cultural geography.
